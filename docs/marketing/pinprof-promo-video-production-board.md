# PinProf Promo Video Production Board

> Status: this board reflects the earlier long-form promo direction. For the current shorter `~1:30` direction, use [pinprof-promo-codex-brief.md](/Users/pillyliu/Documents/Codex/Pinball%20App/docs/marketing/pinprof-promo-codex-brief.md) as the source of truth when there is a conflict.

## Visual System

- **A-roll framing:** Peter on camera. Centered for the opening and closing. Shifted to the left third once app footage begins.
- **B-roll framing:** App footage on the right side of the frame as short stitched clips, never full real-time navigation.
- **Section cards:** Full-screen title card between sections: `Library`, `Practice`, `GameRoom`, `Settings`, `League`.
- **Feature labels:** One 2-3 word label on screen at a time near the app footage.
- **Transitions:** App pane fades in as Peter shifts left. Section cards use quick crossfades. In-section app clips mostly hard cut. After `League`, app pane fades out as Peter returns to center. End on full-screen PinProf logo and fade to black.

## Editing Notes

- Keep each app clip trimmed to the useful proof moment only.
- Avoid showing swipe-backs, waiting, loading states, or long scrolls unless the action itself is the point.
- When one spoken line covers multiple app beats, cut aggressively between beats to keep pace with the voiceover.
- Let the feature label appear just before or exactly when the action becomes obvious.
- Hold the logo end card long enough to breathe, but not so long that the video feels finished before it actually cuts.
