## Shots & Major Playfield Features

The following shots and features define the primary geometry of the playfield. These are listed for orientation only; rules and behavior are defined in the sections that follow.

- **Bronco Loop** — far-left loop
- **Horse Loop / Left Orbit** — left orbit feeding the upper playfield
- **River Adventure Ramp** — left ramp
- **Train Ramp** — center ramp
- **Trick Shooting Loop** — right loop
- **Bank Robbery Ramp** — right ramp
- **Gold Mine** — lock and major feature between ramps
- **Bart Brothers Jump Ramp / Bart Brothers Toy** — upper-right feature
- **Beer Mug Target** — standup target near center-left
- **Bad Guy Drop Targets (4)** — main playfield drop targets
- **Left Gun / Right Gun** — gun assemblies associated with modes
- **Left Inlane / Right Inlane** — inlanes used to start and qualify features

<a id="heading--skillshot"></a>
## Skill Shot

The skill shot awards the item that ends up selected by the spinning ball.

Skill Shot Awards include:

- Light Quick Draw
- Light Gun Fight
- Light Extra Ball
- Light Bounty Award
- Complete Bronco Loop
- Complete River Adventure Ramp
- Complete Train Ramp
- Complete Trick Shooting Loop
- Complete Bank Robbery Ramp
- Increase Rank
- Increase Bonus Multiplier by 3
- 1 Million Points
- Light Lock, or if already lit, Award Lock

Skill Shot awards have been distributed to reduce occurrences of multiple duplicates.

### Upgrade Additions
- Hurry-Up added as the first option in the cup’s rotation.
- **Left Orbit** starts the Hurry-Up at **3M**.
- Successful Hurry-Up collects grow the value of future skill shot Hurry-Ups and the fixed 1M awards.
- Functions as a **Super Skill Shot**.
- Very low-value awards (10K, 50K) are also interspersed in the rotation.

## Bonus Multiplier

The bonus multiplier is increased by:

- Completing the two lanes in the back left corner
- The Bonus × Skill Shot award
- The Bonus × Bounty Award

<a id="heading--extraball"></a>
## Extra Ball

When lit, the Extra Ball is collected by shooting the Gold Mine.

There are several ways to light the Extra Ball:

- Complete Save Polly Peril
- The Skill Shot
- Starting the Showdown Multi-Ball
- Shooting the Beer Mug Target

---

<a id="heading--bounty"></a>
## Bounty

Collect the bounty below the Bart Brothers head.

There are two ways to light the Bounty Award:

- The Lasso Skill Shot
- By defeating a Bad Guy in Quick Draw

The Bounty Award can be any of these things:

- Light Extra Ball
- Light Gun Fight

### Upgrade Additions
- Saloon doors open for qualified Bounty collection shots.

<a id="heading--shoottocollect"></a>
## Shoot to Collect

The Shoot to Collect indicator is a quick reference to the available awards.

- If Extra Ball is lit, shoot for the Gold Mine
- If Bounty is lit, shoot for the Bart Brothers
- If Jackpot is lit, look for the lit Jackpot lights

<a id="heading--gunfight"></a>
## Gun Fight

There are two Gun Fight lights located under each gun.

Gun Fight can be lit in a few ways:

- By the Bounty Award
- By left outlane, when lit
- By beating one of the Bart Brothers

Gun Fight is triggered in the inlane, where the ball is trapped. One of the Bad Guy targets will pop up, and the ball will be released for your shot.

The Bad Guys do not stick around long for a Gun Fight. If you miss, you have just a few seconds to try again.

Winning a Gun Fight increases your rank.

### Upgrade Additions
- Gun Fights are qualified more frequently.
- Gun Fight value builds cumulatively:
  - Every completed Shot Mode and defeated Bart adds to a cached Gun Fight value.
  - First completed Shot Mode awards value at **1×**.
  - Second completed Shot Mode awards value at **2×**.
  - Multiplier continues to increase with each additional completed Shot Mode.
- Defeating a Bart adds to Gun Fight value and increases the multiplier.
- Gun Fight value is lost if the ball ends, or reset when a Gun Fight is completed.
- Gun Fight is also lit by completing a Shot Mode.
- Gun Fight lit inlane insert can be toggled between left and right inlanes.
- Gun Fight is disabled during subsequent Shot Modes.

<a id="heading--rankingup"></a>
## Ranking Up

The light that shows the five ranks is right above the High Noon deputy star.

These three ways increase your rank:

- Bounty Award
- The Lasso Skill Shot
- Winning the Gun Fight

Rank progression:

- Stranger
- Partner
- Deputy
- Sheriff
- Marshal

Your rank affects your score in Quick Draw and High Noon.

### Upgrade Additions

#### Deputy – Gunfight Ball Save
- One-time use; carries between balls.
- Draining out the lit left outlane starts it.
- Shoot the green-lit drop target and avoid the three red-lit drops.
- Shooting a red-lit drop ends the ball.
- This ball save does not collect built Gun Fight value.

#### Marshal – Mini-Wizard Mode
- Phase 1: Single-ball play.
  - Each shot value is tied to one of your individual Gun Fight values.
  - Shoot five jackpots (guarded by Bad Guy drop targets) to bring the train home.
- Phase 2: Marshal Multiball.
  - Hit jackpots and drop targets to capture Bad Guys.

<a id="heading--combo"></a>
## Combo

There is a Combo light on all three ramps and both loops.

- The Combo light is lit for a short time after each ramp or loop shot
- The more Combos you rack up at a time, the faster the Combo lights go out
- Combo is one of the steps towards High Noon
- A ramp or loop hit while the Combo light is on counts toward your combo total
- You must earn 10 Combos to light the Combo on the High Noon badge

---

<a id="heading--beermug"></a>
## Beer Mug

The Beer Mug is located between the Horse Orbit and the River Adventure Ramp.

- Adds two extra balls at 15 and 40 (adjustable) hits
- Next Bank hit increases to 2×, or 3× if Bart is active
- Activates the interactive dropper gameplay mode at 10 and 30 (adjustable) hits
- New High Score for greatest number of Beer Mugs in a game (displays in Attract Mode)

### Upgrade Additions
- Beer Mug shots qualify additional modes:
  - Franks & Beans
  - Drunken Multiball

---

<a id="heading--goldminemb"></a>
## Gold Mine Multi-Ball

Balls are locked in the Gold Mine located between the River Adventure and Train Ramps.

The lock can be lit in a few ways:

- If lock is not lit, a shot to the Gold Mine will light the lock
- The Bounty Award (Bounty can also lock a ball)
- The Lasso Skill Shot

When the third ball is locked, Gold Mine Multi-Ball begins.

Each jackpot can only be hit once until they are all hit. Then they reset and can be hit again.

Every time you hit five jackpots in the same Multi-Ball, the Mother Lode is lit.

New High Score (displays in Attract Mode).

---

<a id="heading--motherlode"></a>
## Mother Lode

- Mother Lode is part of the Gold Mine Multi-Ball
- You must hit five jackpots to light Mother Lode
- To collect Mother Lode, shoot the Gold Mine
- Mother Lode is one of the steps toward High Noon
- Getting at least one Mother Lode will light the badge

---

<a id="heading--savepolly-train"></a>
## Save Polly Peril – Train Ramp (Center Ramp)

The Train Ramp is in the center of the playfield, between the Gold Mine and the Bart Brothers.

To start Save Polly Peril from the Train Ramp, complete the Train Ramp three times.

When Save Polly Peril starts, you have to stop the train and save her. Shooting the Train Ramp will slow the train down so you have more time.

You must shoot the Bank Ramp, the River Adventure Ramp, and the Train Ramp to stop the train and save Polly Peril.

If you save Polly, the Extra Ball will be lit.

---

<a id="heading--savepolly-river"></a>
## Save Polly Peril – River Adventure Ramp (Left Ramp)

The River Adventure Ramp is on the left side, just to the right of the Horse Loop.

To start Save Polly Peril from the River Adventure Ramp, complete the River Adventure Ramp three times.

When Save Polly Peril starts, you have to save Polly Peril from going over the waterfall. Shooting the Train Ramp will slow the train down so you have more time.

You must shoot the Bank Ramp, the River Adventure Ramp, and the Train Ramp to stop the train and save Polly Peril.

If you save Polly, the Extra Ball will be lit.

---

<a id="heading--savepolly-bank"></a>
## Save Polly Peril – Bank Robbery Ramp (Right Ramp)

The Bank Robbery Ramp is on the far-right side.

To start Save Polly Peril from the Bank Robbery Ramp, complete the Bank Robbery Ramp three times.

When Save Polly Peril starts, you have to save Polly Peril from the bank robbers.

Shoot flashing shots to knock bank robbers down. The strong flashing shot indicates which bank robber is holding Polly Peril hostage. Shooting that shot will save Polly Peril.

---

<a id="heading--rideemcowboy"></a>
## Ride ’Em Cowboy

The Horse Loop is on the far left before the River Adventure Ramp.

To start Ride ’Em Cowboy, complete the Horse Loop three times.

During Ride ’Em Cowboy, points are awarded for hits in the jets. To complete Ride ’Em Cowboy, shoot the Horse Loop.

### Upgrade Context
- Counts as a Shot Mode for Gun Fight value building.

---

<a id="heading--marksman"></a>
## Marksman

The Trick Shooting Loop is on the right, between the Bart Brothers and the Bank Robbery Ramp.

To start Marksman, complete the Trick Shooting Loop three times.

During Marksman, shoot lit flashing shots to shoot targets. The strong flashing shot indicates which shot is needed to destroy the target.

Destroying the target improves your Marksman abilities.

### Upgrade Context
- Counts as a Shot Mode for Gun Fight value building.

---

<a id="heading--stampede"></a>
## Stampede Multi-Ball

When the Horse Loop, River Adventure Ramp, Train Ramp, Trick Shooting Loop, and Bank Robbery Ramp have been completed, the Stampede begins.

During the Stampede, all three ramps and both loops are lit for jackpot.

Stampede is one of the steps toward High Noon. Starting Stampede Multi-Ball lights that part of the badge.

---

<a id="heading--bartbrothers"></a>
## Bart Brothers

The Bart Brothers are located in the upper right, between the Train Ramp and the Trick Shooting Loop.

Shoot the jump ramp into the face of the Bart Brothers to attack. You can also sneak a weak shot in from behind the loops.

The Bart Brothers are Big Bart, Sandstorm Bubba Bart, and Bionic Bart.

The Bart Brothers are one of the steps toward High Noon. Defeating the Bart Brothers lights the badge.

### Upgrade Additions
- **Bella Bart** is added; one additional Bart must be defeated before Bionic Bart.
- Saloon doors are closed during Bart fights, blocking the Bart scoop.
- Saloon doors open for qualified Bounty shots, mode starts, or Bart kill shots.
- Bart defeat progress is shown via Saloon character lights.

---

<a id="heading--bionicbart"></a>
## Bionic Bart (Wizard Mode)

Bionic Bart is the final Bart Brother and must be defeated to complete the Bart Brothers on the Sheriff Star.

Bionic Bart requires jackpot shots and successful hits to the Bart Brothers toy.

---

<a id="heading--quickdraw"></a>
## Quick Draw

There are two draw lights located in each inlane.

Quick Draw is started by successfully completing the targets on the right and left side of the playfield. These targets change color state to show how many hits remain to activate Quick Draw.

Quick Draw may also be activated by the Lasso Skill Shot.

Once Quick Draw is enabled, the return lane activates the mode and Bad Guy drop targets appear. The ball does not stop.

If a Bad Guy is defeated, the Bounty is lit according to your rank:

- Stranger: 500,000
- Partner: 750,000
- Deputy: 1,000,000
- Sheriff: 1,500,000
- Marshal: 2,000,000

Points count down from the starting value to 50,000. The faster you shoot, the higher your score.

When you shoot a Bad Guy in Quick Draw, it will stay lit.

Once all four Bad Guy drop targets are defeated, Showdown Multi-Ball is started.

---

<a id="heading--showdown"></a>
## Showdown Multi-Ball

When all four Bad Guys have been shot in Quick Draw, the Showdown begins.

All four Bad Guys pop up to begin the Multi-Ball. As each one is shot, it stays down until all four are shot.

After the fourth Bad Guy is shot, they pop up again and their point value is increased.

Starting Showdown Multi-Ball lights the Extra Ball.

Showdown is one of the steps toward High Noon. Starting Showdown lights that part of the badge.

### Upgrade Additions
- Boss Bart is added to Showdown.
- After completing each set of drops, a lit Jackpot shot fires a shot at Boss Bart.
- Completing all drop sets and Jackpot shots lights a Super Jackpot at Bart.
- Victory Laps follow the Super Jackpot.
- The never-ending Showdown exploit is removed.

---

<a id="heading--highnoon"></a>
## High Noon

When the Sheriff’s Badge is completed, it is time for High Noon.

To complete the Sheriff’s Badge, you must complete:

- Mother Lode
- Showdown
- Combos
- Stampede
- Bart Brothers

When lit, shoot the Gold Mine to start High Noon.

All balls are kicked into play and will be returned for the duration if lost.

All Bad Guy targets pop up. All jackpots are lit.

Shoot **20 Bad Guy targets** during High Noon to win (20 million points).

High Noon is a timed mode. It lasts for 30 seconds, with one additional second added for each Bad Guy target hit during the game, including Bad Guys from Quick Draw, Gun Fights, and Showdown Multi-Ball.

### Upgrade Additions
- High Noon includes a **second stage** following the initial battle.
- Boss Bart escapes with Polly, triggering a final confrontation stage.

<a id="heading--upgrade-modes"></a>
## Upgrade Modes (Additional)

### Saloon Fight
- Qualified by reaching spinner count thresholds.
- Timed mode.
- Switches build value.
- Value is collected once at the Beer Mug.

### Franks & Beans
- Qualified via Beer Mug shots.
- Timed switch frenzy mode.
- All switches score points.

### Drunken Multiball
- Qualified via Beer Mug shots.
- Flippers are reversed.
- Beer Mug is the Super Jackpot.

### Shootout Topper Modes
- Available as topper modes or video modes.
- Shootout 1 and Shootout 2.
- Shoot Bad Guys (and Gals).
- Avoid bystanders.
