## Cheat Sheet

Shoot all four tab shots: **Library**, **Practice**, **League**, and **GameRoom** to qualify jackpots at the PinProf bash toy. Finish all four modes to light **Final Exam** at the saucer. Final Exam is a short timed mode where completed progress converts into a letter grade and a chunky bonus. Pass with flying colors and the game restarts with a playfield multiplier!

<a id="heading--chapter-features"></a>
## Chapter Features

Skill shot: Soft plunge and hit the final exam saucer to score extra credit!

**Library** is the left-loop lesson. A clean orbit spots a reference card and builds **Study Bonus**. Repeating it raises value, but the real purpose is control: the machine wants you to settle in before you chase anything ambitious.

**Practice** lives in the center. Hit the standups or finish the drop bank to log reps and raise the **Confidence Meter**. Practice is solid value, especially when a multiplier is lit, and it is the safest way to turn a messy ball into progress.

**League** is the right-ramp chapter. Shoot it to start a match and raise the **Pressure Meter**. League pays best later in the ball, once the calmer modes have already built up your shot values.

**GameRoom** is the captive-ball mode. Repeated hits give you chances to trade up to rarer machines, which can be collected for big points.

Progress carries over between balls. PinProf allows students to take short snack and bathroom breaks throughout the 4-hour exam. However, beware: PinProf has zero tolerance for cheating. If you tilt, progress resets entirely and you start the exam all over again!

<a id="heading--final-exam"></a>
## Final Exam

Complete all four modes once to light **Final Exam**. Shoot the saucer to start a short timer. During the mode, any lit major shot counts as a correct answer. Alternating the major shots scores better than repeating one shot, so the mode heavily rewards consistency instead of panic flipping. Answer the last question before time expires and you earn a letter grade plus a chunky bonus. A perfect run restarts the game with an added +1 playfield multiplier. Rumor has it the multiplier has no cap. Thought a 4.0 grade was impressive? Try 8.0 or a triple perfect 12.0!!

<a id="heading--scoring-and-strategy"></a>
## Scoring and Strategy

The best strategy is not to spam one shot. Open with Library for control, move into Practice for safe points, cash in League once shot values are built up, and use GameRoom to light new machines.

Remember, don't tilt!
