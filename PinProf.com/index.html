What the app does

PinProf is the go-to pinball app for studying, tracking, and improving.

It helps players understand how they perform in league, what scores to chase on specific games, and where to focus next. Instead of juggling multiple study resources, you get a single place to monitor and record everything you need.

Whether you are preparing for a tournament or just learning a game at home, PinProf gives you tools you need to be the grand champion.
